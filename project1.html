Her every fear

"Her Every Fear" is a riveting thriller that plunges readers into a world of suspense and paranoia. Set in Boston and London, Peter Swanson crafts a gripping narrative following Kate Priddy, who swaps apartments with her cousin and becomes entangled in the dark secrets of her new neighbors. With sharp writing, relentless pacing, and compelling characters, Swanson delivers a psychological thriller that keeps you guessing until the final page. "Her Every Fear" is a must-read for fans of the genre, showcasing Swanson's mastery of suspense and storytelling.
